Water Scarcity Techniques

Rainwater Harvesting

Collecting rainwater from rooftops to store it in tanks or reservoirs for future use. This technique is widely used to conserve water for irrigation and domestic purposes.

Drip Irrigation

Efficient irrigation method that delivers water directly to the plant roots, minimizing waste and optimizing water usage in agriculture.

Greywater Recycling

Reusing water from household sources such as sinks, baths, and washing machines for irrigation and flushing toilets, reducing the demand for fresh water.

Desalination

Process of removing salts and minerals from seawater, making it drinkable. While effective, it can be energy-intensive and expensive.

Aquifer Recharge

Involves replenishing underground water resources by directing surface water into aquifers, ensuring a sustainable supply of groundwater.

Drought-Resistant Crops

Developing crops that require less water, ensuring that agriculture can thrive even in areas with minimal water availability.
